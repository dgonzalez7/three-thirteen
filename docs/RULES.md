# Three-Thirteen Game Rules

## Overview
Three-Thirteen is a multiplayer card game for 4-8 players.

## Game Objective
Players aim to form sets and runs with their cards to score points and avoid being the last player with cards.

## Card Distribution
- Standard 52-card deck
- Each player receives cards based on round number
- Wild cards change each round

## Gameplay Phases
1. **Lobby**: Players gather before game starts
2. **Join Room**: Players enter specific game rooms
3. **Dealing**: Cards are distributed to players
4. **Playing & Scoring**: Players take turns, form sets/runs, score points
5. **Game Finish**: Round ends, scores calculated
6. **Repeat**: Next round begins with new dealing

## Scoring
- Points for sets and runs
- Penalty points for remaining cards
- Wild card bonuses

## Winning
Player with lowest score after final round wins.

## Room Rules
- Maximum 10 concurrent rooms
- 4-8 players per room
- Spectator mode not supported in v1
